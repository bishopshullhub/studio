# **App Name**: Bishops Hull Hub: Connect & Discover

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Activity Schedule: An interactive, mobile-responsive weekly schedule fetched directly from Cloud Firestore, allowing users to easily view activities, filter by day or type, and access contact information.
- Facility Booking Enquiry Form: A streamlined form for users to inquire about hiring the hub, which captures data in Cloud Firestore and triggers automated email notifications to administrators via Firebase Cloud Functions.
- Community Initiatives Showcase: Dedicated content pages for initiatives like 'Join the 100 Club,' 'Buy a Brick,' and 'Sensory Trail,' ensuring all relevant information and external links are easily accessible.
- Community Garden Media Gallery: An optimized image gallery displaying 'Gardens through the seasons' with high-resolution photos served from Firebase Cloud Storage for fast loading times.
- Content Management Interface: A secure administrative portal for managing weekly schedule entries in Cloud Firestore and updating general static content, providing an intuitive backend experience without needing code changes.
- AI-Powered Schedule Entry Assistant: A generative AI tool within the CMS to assist administrators in drafting clear and concise descriptions for new schedule entries or events, streamlining content creation.
- Policy & Information Pages: Standardized, plain-text pages for legal documents (Privacy Statement, Hire Agreement) and essential information (Facility Overview, Hire Prices), enhancing accessibility and SEO.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary color, a muted forest green (#378C7E), provides a sense of community, nature, and reliability. This shade balances approachability with a professional and modern aesthetic, complementing the natural surroundings.
- A very light background color (#EEF6F5) ensures excellent readability and an airy, welcoming feel, echoing the primary hue in a heavily desaturated, near-white tone suitable for a light theme.
- The accent color, a vibrant, optimistic green (#56E073), is chosen to highlight calls-to-action and important interactive elements. Its brighter, more saturated quality creates effective contrast against the primary and background colors, drawing user attention without overwhelming the design.
- Headline and body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its exceptional readability, modern appearance, and versatility across various content types, ensuring a clean and objective presentation for all information.
- Utilize clean, modern line-art style icons. Icons should visually reinforce navigation items, quick links, and calls-to-action, such as a calendar for the schedule or a location pin for contact details.
- A clean, spacious layout emphasizing clarity and intuitive navigation. The homepage will feature a prominent hero section with calls-to-action and 'Quick Access Cards' to key sections, ensuring mobile responsiveness and ease of use.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful animations for smooth user feedback. This includes gentle transitions during form submissions, filtering schedule content, and engaging hover effects on interactive elements, enhancing the modern user experience without being distracting.
